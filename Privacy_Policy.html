Privacy Policy for the Koko's Economy Discord bot/application.

I. Introduction

Welcome to Koko's Economy! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

II. Data Collection

Our bot only collects the following public information:

- User ID

This information is used solely for the purpose of registering users and saving their data within the bot.

III. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

- Legitimate Interest: We collect and process your User ID to provide the services offered by our bot.

IV. Data Usage

The collected User ID is used for:

- Registering users
- Saving user data for bot functionality

V. Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure that your data is stored securely and protected against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Your data is stored for as long as necessary to provide our services.

VI. User Rights

Under Norwegian law and the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- Right to Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
- Right to Rectification: You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
- Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions:
-- I. All your data will be deleted, meaning all your progress will be lost.
-- II. You accept that your data will be gone forever.
-- III. You will not hold developers, managers or other responsible for the loss of your data.
-- IV. Failure to abide by the conditions above will result in indefinite loss of access to our services.
- Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your data under certain circumstances.
- Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.

VII. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at ephyryon@gmail.com or our main communications server: Koko Economy Central.

VIII. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through our Discord bot or other communication channels.
